üí∞ Financial Support for Escaping Violence (2026)

1. The "Leaving Violence Program" (National)

Up to $5,000 total

This replaced the old EVP in mid-2025.

  • $1,500 cash for immediate needs.
  • $3,500 goods/services (removalists, bond, furniture).

Eligibility: 18+, experiencing financial stress, and leaving a violent partner.

How to apply: Call 1800 253 283 or visit leavingviolenceprogram.org.au

2. Centrelink "Crisis Payment"

~1 week's payment amount

A one-off payment for sudden changes in living circumstances.

Eligibility: Must contact Centrelink within 7 days of the incident or leaving home.

How to apply: myGov > Centrelink > Claims > Crisis Payment.

3. Victims of Crime "Financial Assistance Scheme"

Up to $60,000

Victorian state scheme for recovery from violent crime (covers medical, counselling, and lost earnings).

How to apply: victimsofcrime.vic.gov.au or 1800 161 136.

4. Flexible Support Packages (FSPs)

Tailor-made funds for safety (locks, cameras, car repairs, school uniforms).

How to get it: You must be working with a Family Violence Case Manager to apply.

5. Fines Victoria: Family Violence Scheme

Unpaid fines caused by family violence can be withdrawn.

How to apply: Call 1300 019 983 or visit Fines Victoria website.
